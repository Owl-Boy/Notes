# Ordinary Generating Functions
**Ordinary Generating Functions** are a type of [[Generating Functions]] that are of the form 
$$
F(x) = \sum\limits_{n\ge0} f(n)x^{n}
$$
where $f(n)$ is the Generating Functions.
